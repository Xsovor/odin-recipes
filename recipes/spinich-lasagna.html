Spinich Lasagna

Description

This vegitarian lasagna is a favorite of my wife. she loves this dish, especially in fall she preferes a nice white bechamel instead of a red marinara. read on below to find out more.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • fresh spinish (1 large bag)
  • mozarella cheese
  • bechamel
  • Potatoe (cooked)
  • italian seasoning

Steps

  1. pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. pre boil the lasagna noodles in large pot.
  3. cook off the water from the spinish. add italian seasoning.
  4. lay down layers of noodles, followed by potatoe, spinich, bechamel, and cheese.
  5. cook in an oven at 350 degrees for 45 minutes
