Grilled Cheese

Description

This may be a simple sounding recipe, but if you do it right this can be one of the best meals ever. make sure to use a nice thick cut of soft bread and a quality cheese (or two) for ultimpe cheesy goodness.

Ingredients

  • Thick sliced bread
  • quality cheese
  • mayo

Steps

  1. pre heat your skillet on medium heat
  2. spread mayo on one half of your slice, place it on he pre-heated pan
  3. add your cheese (you can let it hang over the bread's edge)
  4. place second slice of bread with mayo up. add a sprinkle of salt
  5. Flip the sandwich after a couple of minutes
  6. Enjoy!
